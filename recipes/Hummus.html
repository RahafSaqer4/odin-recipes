Hummus

Description

Indulge in the smooth and savory flavors of our homemade hummus, a perfect blend of chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. Served with a colorful array of fresh vegetables like crisp cucumbers, juicy carrots, and vibrant bell peppers, this classic Middle Eastern dip is a healthy and delicious snack or light meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup water, or more as needed for desired consistency
    • additions for extra flavor:
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • A pinch of cayenne pepper
  • A few sprigs of fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients:In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, salt, and water.
  2. Blend:Process the mixture until it reaches your desired consistency. You may need to add more water for a smoother texture.
  3. Add Optional Ingredients (Optional):For extra flavor, add the extra virgin olive oil, cayenne pepper, and chopped parsley to the food processor and blend until combined.
  4. Serve:Transfer the hummus to a bowl and serve immediately with pita bread, vegetables, or crackers.
Enjoy your homemade hummus!
